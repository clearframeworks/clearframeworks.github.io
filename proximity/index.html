Online collaboration. No site visits required. Same photo angle and structures — landscape changes only.

Online landscape plans for homeowners

Send clear photos of your front yard, backyard, or problem areas, plus a short note on what you want to change. I build a realistic concept you can evaluate before you commit money to materials, contractors, or installation.

Remote design support for installers

If you install but don’t offer in-house design, Proximity can act as a design layer you plug into your process — helping you sell cleaner scopes, reduce change orders, and close larger jobs with less uncertainty.

Photo concept visuals are built from your real property photo — the camera angle stays the same and permanent structures are not altered. When you need a full layout and measurement-driven planning, true 3D design is available as a separate service.
