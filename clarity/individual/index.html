What this is

Clarity is a quiet place to sort through a situation when everything feels tangled. Not coaching. Not motivation. Not a miracle solution. Just a way to slow down, name what’s real, and separate urgency from noise.

Money

Money & stability

Personal

Life decisions

Moves, relationships, and hard choices where every option has a cost.

How it works

  1. You send context (what’s happening + what’s at stake)
  2. We sort what’s binding you (money, time, obligations, risk, energy)
  3. You get a plan focused on stability, reversibility, and the next moves

If you’re not sure what to include, send what you have — we’ll ask targeted questions.

What you can expect

  • A clear picture of what’s actually driving the problem
  • Options that match your real situation
  • A short plan you can execute this week
  • Adjustments if the situation changes

What happens

We work in two modes depending on how urgent things are and how much support you want.

  • Ongoing support for active situations that evolve week to week
  • One-time deep dive for a specific decision or fork

Details are handled directly by email so we don’t trap you in a one-size-fits-all package.

Integrity

Clarity doesn’t charge people in immediate survival crisis. If you’re facing eviction, domestic violence, or a medical emergency, the right move is local assistance first.

  • Emergency services: call 911 if you’re in immediate danger
  • If you’re in the U.S., dialing 211 can connect you to local resources
  • Once you’re stable, we can help you think cleanly again
