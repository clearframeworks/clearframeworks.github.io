Clarity for public systems.

Clarity is a stability-first framework for mapping barriers, triaging needs, and designing steps that work under real constraints: time, access, paperwork friction, volatility, staffing load, and risk.

What it doesConstraint map → option field → stabilization plan → conditional growth.
Why it helpsTargets the correct constraint instead of escalating the wrong lever.
Agency useIntake triage design, workflow friction review, eligibility/access logic.
BoundaryNot emergency services. Route urgent needs to direct resources first.
