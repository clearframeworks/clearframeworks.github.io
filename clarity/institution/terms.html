Terms / scope

Clarity is structured decision support.

Clarity provides constraint analysis and stability-first decision support. It is not emergency services, and it is not medical, legal, or financial advice.

If someone is in immediate danger or active survival crisis, the correct move is direct services and local emergency resources first.

Clarity is appropriate when meaningful agency still exists: decisions can be made, steps can be taken, and stability can be improved over time.
